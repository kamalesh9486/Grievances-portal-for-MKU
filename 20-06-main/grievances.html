Instruction And Documents Requirement For Grievance

> Upload all your tuition fees paid receipts / challans.


> Upload all your received mark statements, hall tickets / fees paid challans / receipts, result published details and course completed certificate.

Note: If you are not having the course completion certificate first you apply for Course Completion Certificate (CCC).


> Upload your hall ticket(s) / fees paid challans / receipts.

> Upload your received mark statements and results published in website.

> Mention the subject codes, center codes and other details clearly in the application.


> If you are applying to know your results upload your hall ticket(s) and fees paid challans / receipts.

> Give all your exam written details clearly in grievance portal.


> Upload your filled in application together with the Fee Receipt and Xerox copies of Course Completion Certificate / Transfer Certificate and Final Mark Statement.

> Provisional Certificate will not be issued to candidates who have already obtained the Degree (Convocation) Certificate.

> Duplicate provisional certificate will not be issued on any account.

> Provisional Certificates will be issued only for current year students.

> The prescribed fee for obtaining Provisional certificates for all courses except Ph.D., is Rs.360/-

Note: If you are not having the Course Completion Certificate(CCC) and Consolidated Mark Statement (CMS) first you apply for CCC and CMS.
